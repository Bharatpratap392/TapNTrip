# 🧓🏼🙏 Elderly Pilgrimage

Elderly Pilgrimage is a user-friendly platform designed to help senior citizens and their families plan safe and comfortable spiritual journeys to India's most sacred destinations. The platform ensures ease of access, proper health support, group travel options, and all necessary travel details — all tailored specifically for the elderly.

---

## 🌟 Features

- 🧭 Curated pilgrimage packages (e.g., Vaishno Devi, Kedarnath, Varanasi, Rameshwaram)
- 🚌 Comfortable, elderly-friendly transport options
- 🏥 Health & safety support (hospitals nearby, emergency contacts, travel insurance)
- 📅 Real-time calendar of upcoming pilgrimage events
- 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Group/family booking and coordination
- 🔔 Smart reminders for medicines, documents, and trip schedules
